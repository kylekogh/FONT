A Game of Spirits & Spark

◆ The premise

Two lives. One city.
Both will cost you.

Morning: a name, a face, people who need you. Night: the Spark, the bond, the dungeon. Sora holds both. Eventually they find each other.

Morning · The life above

Someone is waiting. The civilian life is real — not a cover, not optional. What you owe it follows you into the dark.

Night · The life below

Eight degrees colder at the threshold. One delve per shift. Come back different, or don't come back.

◈ The city

Sora

Built in rings around a dungeon it was never quite ready to explain. The weight of the deep comes up through the streets. Most people stopped noticing.

  • Outer House of Sora One temple, two sects, four hundred years of disagreement. The hum in the walls is old.
  • Mid · W Guild Hall Every registered binder. Every outstanding bounty. You can feel the records on you when you walk in.
  • Mid · E Titan No sign. No welcome. Find it by finding people who walk like the fight is already over.
  • Inner Ashfields Cheapest rents in Sora. Closest to Mugen. Most people made their peace with that. Mostly.
  • Centre Mugen The dungeon. The thing the city was built around and never quite admits. Nobody lives there. Everybody needs it.
◆ The factions

Who shapes the city

01
The Guild

The clearinghouse between power and the people who need it pointed at something. Merit absolute. Has been here longer than anyone's records agree on.

The weight of being correctly evaluated by someone who doesn't care about you.

02
Ryuugenism

One faith, one building, two sects in four hundred years of disagreement. The Cult reveres the deep. The Veneration reveres the will. They vote as one anyway.

Two certainties running through the same walls.

03
Titan

No ideology. Just the dungeon, and whether you're strong enough to deserve it. Their leader has worn suppressors for nine years. Nobody asks anymore.

The pressure-change of proximity to something that knows exactly what it can do.

04
The Weld

The only workshop in Sora that makes spirit-infused items. Not a shop — a commission. They make for anyone. A Builder's feelings enter the work without exception. Their craft cannot lie.

The warmth of something made with genuine care that still carries it.

05
The Kindled

The largest faction of fighters in Sora. No politics, no dungeon — they face each other. Combat is how you walk the direction your Spark already points. The city comes to watch. Expression, not domination.

The good ache of a stance you refuse to give up.

◆ The bond

The spirit is your Spark made real.
Not a tool. A relationship.
Someone at your back, going out swinging together.

It can break. In the instant of breaking, it can catch. It almost never does. When it does, it is the loudest thing in the room.

"What we feel we protect. What we protect we become."
