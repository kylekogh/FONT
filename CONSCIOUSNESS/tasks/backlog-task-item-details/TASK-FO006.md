# TASK-FO006: Author 30 distinct cards with correct effects

## What

Write and implement 30 distinct cards (C-3). These can include the 15+ Kindled cards from STORY-FO005 plus neutral/base cards. Each card must have a working effect, not just a visual placeholder.

## Done when

- **C-3 satisfied:** At least 30 cards exist in the card pool with distinct, correctly functioning effects
- No two cards are functionally identical (different names on the same effect does not count)
- All 30 cards are playable in the draft screen and can appear in the player's deck
- Card effects cover a range of categories: damage, block, status, draw, energy manipulation, etc.
- At minimum, 15 of the 30 are Kindled faction cards (satisfies FONT-1 overlap)

## Notes

Aim for variety: the 30-card pool should give the player meaningful draft decisions, not just bigger/smaller numbers. At least one card per major effect type. Coordinate with TASK-FO017 (Kindled card authoring) — the Kindled cards written there count toward this 30.
