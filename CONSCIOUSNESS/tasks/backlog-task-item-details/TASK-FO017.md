# TASK-FO017: Author 15+ Kindled cards with faction identity and flavor text

## What

The Kindled's card set: at least 15 cards that feel distinctly Kindled — mechanically, aesthetically, and in flavor text. These cards are the heart of FONT-1 and define what it means to play the demo's starting faction. Implements FONT-1.

## Done when

- **FONT-1 satisfied:** At least 15 Kindled cards exist with correct effects and FONT-canon flavor text
- Cards reflect the Kindled philosophy: combat as the path to expression, not domination
- Card names are in FONT vocabulary — no generic fantasy terms
- Flavor text is in FONT voice: aphantasia-precise, Soran sensory register, no scaffolding references
- Mechanical variety: at minimum attack cards, skill cards, and one power card
- All 15+ count toward the 30-card total (TASK-FO006)

## Kindled card design principles

- Cards should feel like a fighter pushing toward their next form
- Heat in the chest, earned ache, the weight of a stance — this is the sensory register
- Ardour (the Kindled standing track) is a design hook: cards that reward sustained combat, high-output turns, or staying in a fight
- The King's Sight (Kuregar's spirit) could inspire a card about reading the moment ahead
- The Star Path dojo could appear in flavor text as the place where technique was earned

## Notes

**Strict FONT rule:** no references to real-world franchises, other games, or genre conventions appear in any in-app text. The Kindled are not "like MHA heroes" in a tooltip. They are Kindled fighters of Sora. Write in the world's own terms only. See FONT_lore.md method note: "References are calibration, never content."
