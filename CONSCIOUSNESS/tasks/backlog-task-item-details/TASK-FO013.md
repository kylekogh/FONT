# TASK-FO013: Design boss encounter with multi-phase or unique mechanic

## What

The demo's climax. A boss enemy that does something no regular encounter does — a phase transition, a unique mechanic, a rule that changes mid-fight. This is the moment the run has been building toward. Implements RUN-2.

## Done when

- **RUN-2 satisfied:** One boss encounter exists with a multi-phase or unique mechanic
- The boss has at least 2 phases OR one mechanic that doesn't appear in any standard encounter
- Phase transition is communicated clearly to the player (intent display, visual shift, or explicit announcement)
- The boss fight is balanced to be winnable with a reasonable Kindled deck but requires the player to have engaged with the game's mechanics
- Defeating the boss leads to the victory screen (TASK-FO019)

## Notes

Design to FONT tone: the boss should feel like a fighter who took the path too far, not a generic RPG monster. Lore-wise, a Limit-Seeker who went past their rank into the Uncharted and came back wrong, or a Blaze-rank Kindled fighter whose expression became destructive, both fit. The mechanic should feel like something a person in Sora would actually do with a Spark — not a magical effect from nowhere.
