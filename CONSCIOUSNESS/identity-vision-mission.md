## Identity

FONT is a roguelike deckbuilder set in the city of Sora — a world where every person is born with a Spark, the dungeon beneath the city is both adversary and inheritance, and your deck is an expression of who you actually are.

The world is already written. The game is the proof that it translates.

## Vision

A fully playable game where FONT's lore is the mechanic. Factions become deck identities. Spirits become card archetypes. The bond becomes the thing you're fighting to protect. The dungeon is not a setting — it is the central character.

Accessible everywhere. Mobile-first. With a meta-progression layer that rewards the long arc, and an AI companion who knows the world and can meet the player inside it — so no one ever feels alone in Sora.

## Mission

1. **Ship the demo** — a feature-complete roguelike deckbuilder demo set in the FONT universe, playable from a fresh open to a run-end (win or death), anchored in The Kindled as the first faction. Proves the world is a game worth playing. *(DIRECT-FO001)*

2. **Grow it into a full game** — Play Store distribution, all five factions playable, a roguelite meta-progression layer where each loss earns something for the next run. The demo becomes the spine of a real shipped product. *(DIRECT-FO002)*

3. **Bring in the companion** — an in-game AI that knows the FONT world, speaks in its voice, and responds to what happens in the run. The lore is the prompt. The player gets a partner who was always there.
